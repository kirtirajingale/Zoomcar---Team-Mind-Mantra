About Host by Zoomcar and
the car

Host by Zoomcar is a unique community vehicle pooling program for Car owners to let them share their cars when it's not in use on Zoomcar platform to earn money and rewards(upto Rs. 10,000/-* joining bonus and booking revenue).

Visit the Host by Zoomcar website, select your city, login, fill a form with basic details and agree to terms & conditions

You can list any car model ranging from hatchbacks to SUVs.

About Payment, Offers and Deposit

So far, you were being paid by Zoomcar for listing your vehicle on the platform based on the number of hours you listed it for, on an hourly rate. In the revenue share model, you will be eligible to obtain 60% of the booking revenue that your vehicle earns with every booking, paid out weekly, for bookings ending in that particular week.

While your earning potential in the revenue share model depends on your listing frequency, bookings obtained by your vehicle and your rating, most hosts who list.

Your overall rating is the average of all ratings that you receive from guests who use your car on the Zoomcar platform.
